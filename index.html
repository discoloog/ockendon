A Celebration of Short Films

Ockendon 42 is an independent short film festival, born from a summer in Assos, Turkey. Join us in London for an inspiring showcase of creativity.

About the Festival

Ockendon 42 Film Festival is an intimate gathering of film lovers and creators, celebrating short films from around the world. Organized by a group of friends, the festival was conceived during a summer vacation in Assos, Aegean Turkey.
